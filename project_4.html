004 : Android E-Commerce App- Alicisindan

Alicisindan is an e-commerce app that enables both the buyer and the seller to create listings for products they desire to buy/sell. It is a comprehensive app that allows users to reach out to other users, and buy or sell a diverse set of products. The key difference between conventional e-commerce apps and Alicisindan is that with Alicisindan, buyers can also announce the products they are looking for, enabling potential sellers to reach out to them. In addition, with the forum feature of the app, users will be able to share reviews and recommendations. The project is made possible with my teammates Can Tucer, Emirhan Yagcıoglu, Bertan Turgut, and Begum Oz.
